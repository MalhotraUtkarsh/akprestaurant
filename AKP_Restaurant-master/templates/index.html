The History

The Allahabad Kabab Paratha is in the restaurant business since 1942. Start with just one person Now we are grown to a happy family of 15 employees and hundreds of customers. We always have one belief and that is 'We trust in our quality', We trust that the quality of our meals will bring joy and happiness to your face. This initiative of the well being of our customers kept us going all these years. Now we hope that you will continue to shower us with your deep love and affection for the upcoming years and we will shower your bellies with the most delicious food in Allahabad.

We are glad to tell you that our menu is filled with a lot of mouth-watering dishes that can satisfy your cravings. But If you are new to our restaurant. We humbly request you to try 'Seekh Kawab', one of our most selling dishes and You will love it.
